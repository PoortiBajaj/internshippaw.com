--- Come and Seek ---

Millions of Youth around the world struggle to get quality education and job opportunities. They face barriers such as lack of permission to travel, funds and guidance. These barriers keep our coming Generation out of the workforce and make them dependent on others. Come and Seek Careers is a mobile platform set up to remove barriers for our youth to get quality career guidance in photography, skills and job opportunities. Whether you are a student or job-seeker, you get help to create your identity from this portal.
